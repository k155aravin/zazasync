Questions
Everything you need to know.
Is this legal? Are you sharing individual user data?▼
No individual user data is ever shared. All data is aggregated and anonymized — you receive statistics, trends, and breakdowns, never individual profiles. This is the same model used by Nielsen, Spotify, and Google Audience Insights. We are fully compliant with Quebec Law 25 and PIPEDA.
How many users do you have? Is the data statistically significant?▼
We are in active growth. Our free sample report shows the exact sample size for your brand's data so you can judge statistical significance before committing. Brands with more SQDC presence naturally accumulate more data signals faster.
Are you affiliated with the SQDC?▼
No. ZazaSync is a completely independent platform. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to the Société québécoise du cannabis in any way. We are an independent data intelligence company.
How is this different from SQDC sales data?▼
SQDC sales data tells you what sold. ZazaSync tells you who wanted it, where they are, how old they are, what language they speak, and how urgently they wanted it — before the sale happens. That's demand intelligence vs lagging sales data. Completely different, and complementary.
Can I cancel anytime?▼
Yes. All plans are month-to-month with no long-term commitment. Cancel before your next billing date and you won't be charged again. We believe the data speaks for itself — you'll want to stay.
Can my marketing agency subscribe on behalf of multiple brands?▼
Yes — that's exactly what our Pro plan is designed for. One subscription, multiple brand dashboards, one dedicated account manager. Contact us to discuss agency pricing.
